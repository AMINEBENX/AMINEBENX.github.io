Who is Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who is best known for developing the theory of relativity.

List of some his discoveries :

  1. Photoelectric Effect (1905)
  2. Special Theory of Relativity (1905)
  3. Mass-Energy Equivalence (E=mc2) (1905)
  4. Explanation of Brownian Motion (1905)
  5. General Theory of Relativity (1915)
